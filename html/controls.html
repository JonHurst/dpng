Keyboard

KeyAction
j or downMove both highlights down one line.
k or upMove both highlights up one line.
h or homeMove both highlights to first line.
n or shift+j or shift+downMove the image highlight down one line.
shift+k or shift+upMove the image highlight up one line.
e or iEdit the text.
ESCClose the editor and re-validate.

Mouse

  • Clicking in the the text pane moves the highlight in both panes.
  • Clicking in the image pane moves the highlight in that pane only.
  • Clicking on the highlighted line in the text pane opens the editor.
